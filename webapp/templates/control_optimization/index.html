Overview

Control & Optimization in PINNs leverages the differentiable nature of physics-informed neural networks to solve optimal control problems and design optimization tasks. The end-to-end differentiability enables efficient gradient-based optimization for complex physical systems.

Key Features:

  • Differentiable Control: End-to-end differentiable control policies
  • Optimal Design: Gradient-based design optimization
  • Real-time Control: Fast control computation once trained
  • Multi-objective Optimization: Handle multiple competing objectives
